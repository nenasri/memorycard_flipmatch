Follow these steps to enjoy Memory Card: Flip & Match!
  1. Click the "Start" button on the home page to begin.
  2. Flip any two cards by clicking on them.
  3. If the emojis match, they will stay flipped and you earn points!
  4. If they do not match, the cards will flip back. Remember their position!
  5. Try to match all pairs as fast as possible to earn more stars.
  6. Check your score, time, and stars at the top info bar.
  7. Complete the game to unlock achievements and progress to the next level.
